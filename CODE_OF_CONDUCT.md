# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want Cadiax to be an open, respectful, and technically serious project. Contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to interact professionally and constructively.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and direct
- assume good intent, but challenge ideas clearly
- focus feedback on behavior, design, code, and evidence
- keep discussions relevant to the project
- protect private information, credentials, and user data

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- publishing private data, secrets, or exploit details irresponsibly
- derailing technical discussions with hostility or bad-faith behavior
- spam, trolling, or repeated low-signal disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or moderate content that violates this policy. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent removal from the project spaces.

## Reporting

For general conduct concerns, open an issue or discussion if the topic is safe to discuss publicly.

For sensitive issues, especially those involving privacy or security, use the reporting path in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
